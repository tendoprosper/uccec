About UCCEC ✨

The Uganda Chamber of Commerce & Entrepreneurs' League Confederation (UCCEC) is dedicated to promoting sustainable plant-based agriculture, entrepreneurship, investment, trade, and environmental development across Uganda.

With a mission to uplift farmers, support entrepreneurs, and create lasting economic growth through green practices, UCCEC brings together stakeholders in Uganda's thriving sustainable agriculture sector.

We believe in the power of sustainable farming, fair trade practices, and eco-friendly methods to transform lives and build a greener future for our communities.
